NAKED LIES

"Naked Lies" is a podcast that delves deep into the unspoken truths of life. Each episode features a take on personal experiences of struggling through the ups and downs of life. From heartbreak to financial struggles, the podcast aims to explore the raw realities of what it means to be human. We meet a young woman who describes herself as "Just a lonely girl living in lonely streets with empty pockets and a bag full of dreams." She opens up about the challenges she's faced as she's pursued her dreams, from financial hardship to loneliness and isolation. But even in the face of adversity, she's maintained her determination and drive, using her hardships as fuel to keep pushing forward. Join us for raw and honest conversation(s) about life's struggles, as we explore the naked truths behind the lies we tell ourselves and others. Tune in to "Naked Lies" to hear inspiring stories.
